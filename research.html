I am a microbial ecologist which means that I am interested in the role of microorganisms on their natural environments, how they contribute to the biogeochemical cycles, how they interact with other organism (specially plants) and how can we use them for bioremediation and other applications.

   My research has been greatly influenced by my time in Michigan State Univerrsity working at the Center for Microbial Ecology at Michigan State University and the Ribosomal Database Project.
